You hit a dead end with dc3dd, and it’s not because you’re stupid — it’s because your dc3dd build is refusing the dd-style keywords (bs=, count=). You can waste hours brute-forcing syntax, or you can be smart and stop.

What that error means

unrecognized option bs=... / unrecognized option count=... = your dc3dd binary is not accepting those parameters on this system. So you can’t do the “small sample copy” demo the way we planned.
